🏛️ About Toledo Tour

A local travel guide to Toledo, Spain — created by people who love this city and have spent years sharing it with visitors from every corner of the world.

🎯 Our Mission

Toledo Tour is an online travel guide dedicated exclusively to Toledo, Spain. Our mission is to help travellers from around the world discover Toledo authentically: beyond the crowded tourist circuits and clichéd itineraries, towards the real city that those of us who know it love.

Toledo has been a UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1986 and is one of the historically richest cities in Europe. Yet most travel guides treat it as a day trip from Madrid. We believe Toledo deserves more: more time, more context, and a deeper understanding of who built it, why, and what it still means today. This guide is our way of giving that to the visitors who come to explore it.

All content on Toledo Tour is created by contributors with direct knowledge of the city: current and former Toledo residents, licensed local guides, and specialists in medieval history, Mudéjar art and Spanish Golden Age culture. We review and update our information regularly to keep it accurate, with particular attention to entrance prices, opening hours, and transport schedules.

Nature and Activities Team

Outdoor enthusiasts with first-hand experience of the hiking routes in the Montes de Toledo, birdwatching at Barrancas de Burujón, and water activities on the Tagus. They provide accurate, seasonal trail condition reports and practical advice for nature visitors.

English Content Team

English-language content is written specifically for international visitors by tourism-specialist writers with Toledo residency experience. Our English pages are not machine translations: they are written with the specific needs of the anglophone traveller in mind — visas, currency, arrival from airport, accessibility for English speakers, and contexts that Spanish visitors take for granted but international visitors need explained.

📋 How We Create Content

Verification Policy

All practical visitor information (monument entrance prices, opening hours, transport fares, festival dates) is verified directly against official sources: individual monument websites, Renfe (national rail operator), ALSA (coach operator), and the Toledo City Council official tourism portal. Prices and schedules are reviewed at minimum twice yearly.

Update Policy

Content is updated to reflect price changes, new openings or closures, changes in monument visiting policies, and special events. We indicate the reference year in our content (currently 2026). If you find inaccurate information, please contact us — community corrections help keep the guide current for everyone.

Editorial Independence

Toledo Tour is an independent editorial website. We are not a booking platform and do not receive commissions, sponsorship payments or free services in exchange for recommendations of hotels, restaurants or activities. All recommendations are based solely on the knowledge and direct experience of our contributors.

The advertisements appearing on this site are managed by Google AdSense and are selected automatically based on your browser history and our content. They do not represent editorial endorsement of any specific product or service by Toledo Tour.
